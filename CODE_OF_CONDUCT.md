# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Neonplex is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of identity, background, experience, ability, or point of view.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing the actual impact of words and actions.
- Accept feedback gracefully and correct mistakes promptly.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy, attribution, licenses, and project boundaries.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, or other abusive conduct are not
acceptable.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions,
and other project spaces. It also applies when someone publicly represents the
project or its community.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns to project maintainer
[@VictorZakharov](https://github.com/VictorZakharov). If a private maintainer
contact channel is not available, open a minimal issue requesting private
contact without including sensitive details. GitHub's moderation and abuse
reporting tools are also available for conduct that violates GitHub policy.

Maintainers will review reports as privately and promptly as practical. They
may edit or remove contributions, issue a warning, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban a participant, depending on the nature and
pattern of the behavior. Retaliation against someone who makes a good-faith
report is prohibited.

This policy follows the principles of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
